Introduction

Our Mission

  • Build a Next-Generation Multimodal Ecosystem
    • We develop new data collection pipelines to gather temporally aligned multimodal data. We not only process common data like 2D or mono audio; we also process data that enables immersive experiences, such as 4D and spatial audio.
    • We design new model architectures to process this multimodal data. Our system can reason, retrieve, generate, and edit multimodal content.
    • Our models enable human-perception-like experiences for content creation, XR/gaming, and robotics.
  • Competitive Moat
    • Not training-free: we train and fine-tune models for superior quality.
    • We define new representations for different data types.
    • Well-established pipelines for multimodal data generation and cleaning.
    • A foundation model with multimodal inputs and outputs, with native support for iterative multimodal editing.

Why us?

  • An end-to-end, trainable multimodal generation and editing engine; a proof-of-concept paper is coming soon.
  • A foundation model with multimodal inputs and outputs, with native support for interactive sessions.
  • Our team has diverse expertise in audio, video, music, and NLP, with papers accepted at NeurIPS, ICLR, ISMIR, KDD, and ICASSP.

Multimodal Data For Creatiave Generation

We curate paired, long to short multimodal data for film, with clean, time-aligned tracks for dialogue, music, and visuals. These pairs enable high-fidelity generation and distillation from long footage into polished short-form assets while preserving temporal synchronization and providing supervision to learn temporal correspondence in film settings.
